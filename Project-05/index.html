Anirudh Chandar is an architectural designer and researcher from India with professional and academic experience distributed between India, Singapore and New York. He is currently a Columbia University student in the Master of Architecture program expected to graduate in May 2021. He received his Bachelor of Arts in Architecture Degree at the National University of Singapore in 2014.
Having professionally worked on competitions, a constructed exhibition space in Paris, several constructed projects in India and a research-design-build project in Singapore, his interest is in combining ideas on sustainability, material innovations, structural articulation and equity in his design methodology in service of producing exciting and innovative architecture. With a focus on conceptual and schematic design, his professional goal is to develop his skill-set to engage and contribute to the design and execution of large scale public projects.
